One-handed Swords are the primary weapons of the Knight, Time Battlemage and Bushi job classes.

Greatswords (Two-Handed Swords)

The strongest weapons in the game (in most cases). Two-handed Swords are the primary weapons of Knights but can also be used by Red Battlemages and White Mages.
